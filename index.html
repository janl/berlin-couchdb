Berlin.JS is a usergroup focused on Javascript and related topics. We meet regularly on the 3rd Thursday each month at 7p.m. at co.up Offices, Adalbertstraße 7-8 in Berlin-Kreuzberg.

Mathias Schäfer @molily

Proper graphing with Raphaël

Matt Patterson @fidothe

Still scarred from graph-wrangling the Birmingham Civic Dashboard, Matt spelunked deep inside Raphaël and gRaphaël and lived to tell the tale.

He'll run through graphing with Raphaël, whether you need gRaphaël, and how to roll your own.

The Node.js Scalability Myth

Felix Geisendörfer @felixge

This talk will explore the idea of node.js being more scalable than other technologies, how much of it is true, and how much you should care.
